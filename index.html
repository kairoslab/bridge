Hi! My name is Juan Pablo.

I help students build confidence in mathematics.

Background & Credentials

I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Pure Mathematics from the University of North Carolina Asheville, with a minor concentration in Data Science. In 2026 I received the Outstanding Citizenship Award from the Mathematics and Statistics Department at UNCA — an immense honor that I hope to live up to.

What I Teach

My research interests are in Mathematical Biology and Dynamical Systems, and I am currently preparing for graduate studies in those areas. I am drawn to modeling the natural world in an interdisciplinary capacity and to using mathematics as a means for science communication.

I work with students from middle school through early college in algebra, geometry, calculus, linear algebra, and statistics, as well as in an introduction to proof-based mathematics and general computer and data science literacy.

Beyond Mathematics
