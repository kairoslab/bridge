Hi! My name is Juan Pablo.

I help students build confidence in mathematics.

Background & Credentials

I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Pure Mathematics from the University of North Carolina Asheville, with a minor concentration in Data Science. In 2026 I received the Outstanding Citizenship Award from the Mathematics and Statistics Department at UNCA — an immense honor that I hope to live up to.

What I Teach

I work with students from middle school through early college in algebra, geometry, calculus, linear algebra, and statistics, as well as in an introduction to proof-based mathematics and general computer and data science literacy.

My research interests are in Mathematical Biology and Dynamical Systems, and I am currently preparing for graduate studies in those areas. I am drawn to modeling the natural world in an interdisciplinary capacity and to using mathematics as a means for science communication.

That being said, I have a passion for the subject as a whole and how it helps one relate to other disciplines. I want to make mathematics more accessible to everyone, and my interest is in helping students develop strong foundations. I know first hand how much one's community can impact long term success, how one friend can make a world of difference, and that's what I want to be, a companion. I am very certain that with patience, time, and dedication these foundations can be established and will result in life-long benefits in a person's academic studies and professional endeavors.

Beyond Mathematics

Tutoring

If you're a student at UNCA, I'd encourage you first and foremost to make use of the Math Lab — it's an excellent resource, and the community there is the best. If you're looking for something more personal, I'd be glad to hear from you.

I want to make your life easier. When you contact me, I will share a link to organize a meeting with the google calendar scheduler. That way you can see my availability and request a time. Online or in-person, we can have a chat or go ahead and schedule a tutoring appointment.

I also want you to feel like this time is a good investment throughout. So, I would like to offer our first hour gratuitously. Then, my rates as follows: in-person $55/hr and online $45. I think two hour sessions are best, but we can adjust that so it's all copacetic.
